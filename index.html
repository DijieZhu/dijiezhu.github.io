Dijie Zhu

M.S. student in Electrical & Computer Engineering at UCLA. I work on semi‑supervised learning (dynamic thresholds, calibration), wireless in‑band full‑duplex self‑interference cancellation (NN‑aided digital SIC under time‑varying channels), and robotics. I’m broadly interested in calibration/conformal methods for reliability, generative simulation and evaluation, and wireless‑enabled multi‑robot coordination.

You can reach me at dijie@ucla.edu. A copy of my CV is available here (PDF).
