Bio Misc

Dijie Zhu

I’m a second‑year M.S. student in Electrical & Computer Engineering at UCLA. During 2020–2024, I completed my B.Eng. through the UESTC–University of Glasgow joint programme, graduating with UK First Class Honour.

My research interests span semi‑supervised learning, wireless communications, and robotics. My current focus is dynamic thresholding and calibration for semi‑supervised learning—aiming to push simple, efficient models beyond SOTA in classic CV—under Prof. Suhas Diggavi at UCLA. Previously, I worked on signal processing for in‑band full‑duplex self‑interference cancellation. Most recently, I started a project with Prof. Chen Tang on embodied AI evaluation.
