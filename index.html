Dijie Zhu

I’m currently a second year M.S. student in Electrical & Computer Engineering at UCLA. During 2020-2024, I completed my B.Eng. through the University of Electronic Science and Technology of China–University of Glasgow joint programme, graduating with First Class Honours UK.

My research interests span semi-supervised learning, wireless communications, and robotics. My current focus is dynamic thresholding and calibration for semi-supervised learning—aiming to go beyond SOTA in classic CV area, under Prof. Suhas Diggavi at UCLA. Previously, my work centered on signal processing for wireless systems, especially in-band full-duplex self-interference cancellation. Most recently, I started a project with Prof. Chen Tang on embodied AI evaluation.

You can reach me at dijie@ucla.edu. A copy of my CV is available here (PDF).
