Research

I study semi-supervised learning (dynamic thresholding, pseudo-label calibration), wireless in-band full-duplex self-interference cancellation (NN-aided digital SIC under time-varying channels), and robotics (control + vision). Current focus: faster-converging SSL beyond FreeMatch/InfoMatch with better final accuracy.

Broader interests include calibration/conformal methods for reliability, generative simulation and evaluation, and wireless-enabled multi-robot coordination.
